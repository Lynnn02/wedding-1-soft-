How We Met

"The beginning of our love story."

It all started with a smile across the room at a friend's wedding. Aiman was captivated by Sofea's laughter, and Sofea couldn't ignore Aiman's warm gaze. A casual conversation that evening turned into endless late-night calls, shared sunsets, and long drives. Despite the miles between them, their hearts found a way to bridge the distance. From the first coffee date where Aiman spilled his drink out of nervousness to the road trips where Sofea's playlists played on repeat, every moment was a chapter of their story. What began as a simple hello blossomed into a promise to never say goodbye.
